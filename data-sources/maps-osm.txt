# OSM PBF sources for the daily data-refresh workflow.
#
# One region per line:  <slug>  <url>
# - slug is the filename we publish under (e.g. "north-macedonia.osm.pbf")
# - url is fetched verbatim; Geofabrik regional extracts work directly
#
# Lines starting with "#" and empty lines are ignored. The slug
# doesn't have to match the upstream filename — we use the modern
# country name on our side, but Geofabrik may have kept a legacy
# slug for URL stability (see north-macedonia → macedonia below).
#
# After a successful run, files land on the gh-pages branch under
#   /cdn/maps/osm/<slug>.osm.pbf
# and are served by GitHub Pages at
#   https://xhevops-claude.github.io/claude-default/cdn/maps/osm/<slug>.osm.pbf

# North Macedonia — Geofabrik kept the pre-2019 "macedonia" slug
# even though the country renamed; the URL is stable, so we use
# their slug upstream and our preferred slug on our side.
north-macedonia  https://download.geofabrik.de/europe/macedonia-latest.osm.pbf
